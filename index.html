About

Redefining the
Future of Tech

Techfest is IIT Bombay's annual science and technology festival, the largest of its kind in Asia. Since 1998, we've brought together over 180,000 participants from 190+ countries in an electrifying celebration of human ingenuity.

From quantum computing to space exploration, from AI to robotics — Techfest is where the next generation of world-changers come to learn, compete, and be inspired.
